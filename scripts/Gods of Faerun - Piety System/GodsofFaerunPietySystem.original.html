Amaunator

The Keeper of the Eternal Sun, the Light of Law

The rule of law and the glory of the sun both fall in Amaunator’s dominion. He is a stern and unforgiving god, concerned with keeping promises and maintaining order. Amaunator’s priests help establish bureaucracies and order in communities. They witness contracts and signed agreements, signifying these documents’ validity by stamping them with the god’s sun symbol.

Amaunator’s Champions

Alignment: Usually lawful, often neutral

Suggested Classes: Cleric, paladin, fighter, wizard

Suggested Cleric Domains: Light, Order

Suggested Backgrounds: Acolyte, noble, sage, soldier

Earning and Losing Piety

You increase your piety score to Amaunator when you expand the god’s influence in the world in a concrete way through acts such as these:

  • Upholding law
  • Enforcing contracts
  • Exposing corruption
  • Bringing light to darkness

Your piety score to Amaunator decreases if you diminish Amaunator’s influence in the world, contradict his ideals, or let him down through acts such as these:

  • Breaking oaths
  • Subverting law
  • Aiding chaos or deception

Amaunator’s Devotee

Piety 3+ Amaunator trait

You can cast Guiding Bolt with this trait, requiring no material components, a number of times equal to your Intelligence or Wisdom modifier (minimum of once). You regain all expended uses when you finish a long rest. Intelligence or Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

Amaunator’s Votary

Piety 10+ Amaunator trait

You can cast Daylight with this trait, requiring no material components. Once you cast the spell in this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a long rest. Intelligence or Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

Amaunator’s Disciple

Piety 25+ Amaunator trait

When you succeed on a saving throw against being charmed or frightened, you can use your reaction to reflect the effect back on the caster. The caster must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Wisdom modifier) or be affected by the same condition until the end of their next turn.

Champion of the Eternal Sun

Piety 50+ Amaunator trait

You can increase your Intelligence or Wisdom score by 2 and also increase your maximum for that score by 2.
